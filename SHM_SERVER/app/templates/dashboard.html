About the System

The Challenge

Infrastructure in rapidly developing regions faces significant challenges from environmental stress and dynamic loads. Traditional reactive maintenance approaches lead to costly emergency repairs and safety hazards.

Our Solution

This embedded SHM system enables continuous, real-time monitoring of critical structural parameters, supporting early fault detection and proactive maintenance decisions.
